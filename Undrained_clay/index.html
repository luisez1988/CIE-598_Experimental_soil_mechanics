Undrained shear strength

Is the shear strength of a soil sample when the drainage is prevented. In an undrained test water can't scape. Therefore \(\Delta V = 0\).
It is affected by:
  • Volumetric behavior of soil
  • OCR and initial density
  • Initial consolidation stress.
  • Stress path
  • Others in a lesser degree
