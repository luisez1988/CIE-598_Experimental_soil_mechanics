Indirect tests to measure \(S_u\)

There are several laboratory indirect tests to measure ml of clay. These tests are often used as a complement but never as a replacement of proper laboratory tests.
  • These tests are fast and simple.
  • Boundary conditions are not controlled. They are more akin to UU conditions.
  • Could work as a validation benchmark for UUTx.
We will cover the following tests:
  1. Mini vane shear test
  2. Free fall cone test
  3. Torvane test
  4. Pocket penetrometer

Mini vane shear test

A mini vane is pushed into an intact or remolded sample and rotated. The torque and angular displacement are measured.
  • ASTM D4648 available.
  • Based on reliable principles.
  • Can be conducted on intact leftovers of sample.
  • Can be conducted multiple times per sample
From the angular displacement and the spring stiffness you can get the torque. Then apply:
\( S_u= \cfrac{2 T_u}{\pi D^2 (H+D/3)} \)

Free fall cone

A cone is dropped from a fixed height and penetrates the soil. The penetration depth is measured.
  • repeatable results.
  • Standard unavailable.
  • Mechanism of failure consists of dynamic penetration process.
  • Can be conducted multiple times per sample.
