Background

Process of soil densification as water flows out of soil mass as a response to an increase in total stress. It is a time dependent process.

Objective

  1. Determine the virgin compression index (\(C_c\)) and modified compression index (\(C_{εc}\)).
  2. Determine the recompression index (\(C_r\)) and modified recompression index (\(C_{εr}\)).
  3. Determine the stress-dependent coefficient of volume compressivility (\(m_v\)) and the coefficient of compressivility (\(a_v\)).
  4. Determine the preconsolidation pressure (\(σ'_p\)) and the overconsolidation ratio (\(OCR\)).
  5. Determine stress-dependent coefficient of consolidation (\(C_v\)).
  6. Determine stress dependent hydraulic conductivity (\(k\)).
  7. Determine the coefficient of secondary compression (\(C_α\))
